About

I'm an AI researcher at Krutrim AI Labs, working on cutting-edge multilingual language models designed for India's diverse linguistic landscape. My research focuses on making AI accessible to over a billion people through culturally-aware and linguistically-inclusive systems.

Originally from Muzaffarpur, Bihar, I completed my schooling at DAV Public School and DPS Bokaro Steel City before pursuing Computer Science at IIT Guwahati. There, I developed a passion for machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing. My work spans across conversational AI, multi-modal systems, and deep metric learning.

Beyond research, I'm deeply curious about the frontiers of human knowledge and enjoy exploring creative pursuits through photography and visual storytelling.
